Our Story

Built for Filipino brands ready to grow globally

We started Anima Forte Global with a simple conviction: Filipino-made products — from botanical skincare to functional wellness supplements to fitness equipment — are world-class. What they often lack is not quality, but a trusted bridge to international markets.

Our role is to be that bridge. We handle the complexity so our brand partners don't have to: distribution channels, import and export compliance, logistics coordination, and buyer relationships across the US, UK, and UAE.

We measure our success by our partners' growth. When a Filipino manufacturer signs their first US wholesale account, or when a Filipina in London finds a brand she grew up with on a salon shelf — that's what we're building toward.

We're at an early stage, and we're intentional about that. We'd rather grow alongside a small number of truly aligned brand partners than scale without integrity. If your brand is ready to explore international distribution, we'd love to start a conversation.

Jessa C. Macas
Founder & Chief Executive

Jessa brings a business degree and direct international work experience across Asian markets to everything Anima Forte Global does. Her cultural fluency — grounded in lived experience, not assumption — shapes how we build relationships with Filipino manufacturers and how we serve diaspora communities abroad.


Her approach is straightforward: serve the partner and buyer first, and the business follows. She built Anima Forte Global not to be a middleman, but to be a long-term growth partner — someone who understands both where Filipino brands come from and where they can go.


"The goal is never just a transaction. It's making sure every brand in our network has a real future in the markets we open for them."

How We Work

You build great products. We open the markets.

Our process is designed to protect your brand, reduce your risk, and make international distribution feel manageable — not overwhelming.

01

We Listen First

Every partnership starts with understanding your brand, your production capacity, your goals. We don't assume — we ask. This conversation shapes everything that follows.

02

We Map the Path

Together we assess the right markets, MOQ structure, pricing tiers, and compliance requirements. You see exactly where your products would go and how they'd be positioned.

03

We Handle the Complexity

Logistics, customs, buyer negotiations, invoice terms, regulatory compliance — we manage the operational layer so your team stays focused on what it does best: manufacturing.

04

We Grow Together

As your brand scales into new markets, we scale our support. Every account we open, every reorder we close — you see the results. Transparent reporting, shared milestones.

Where We Distribute

Four markets. One community.

The Filipino diaspora is one of the most globally dispersed — and most loyal — consumer communities in the world. We're positioned in four markets where that purchasing power and cultural connection are strongest.

🇺🇸

United States

Over 4 million Filipinos in the US — concentrated in California, Hawaii, New York, and Texas. Filipino salons, wellness clinics, balikbayan stores, and grocery chains are the distribution backbone.

🇬🇧

United Kingdom

A significant and underserved Filipino community, particularly in London and the South East. Strong OFW professional presence with above-average household income and brand loyalty.

🇦🇪

United Arab Emirates

One of the highest concentrations of OFWs globally. Filipino grocery and wellness products have consistent, high-frequency demand in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah.

🌏

East Asia

Japan and Hong Kong host two of the largest Filipino OFW communities in Asia. Strong demand for trusted Filipino wellness, beauty, and food brands in markets with high purchasing power and cultural familiarity.
